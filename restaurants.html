Favorite Restaurants

Suburbs

Kogii Kogii Express - Naperville

You get to build-your-own bibimbop bowls (tons of ingredients to customize)

Katy's Dumplings - Westmont

One of my favorite Chinese restaurants-nice portions and great flavor

Wildberry Pancakes and Cafe - Schaumburg

Their "sweet" options aren't excessively sweet, and their skillets are yummy

Note: Try their berry marscapone !!

Chicago

Sushi Taku

Good price for decent quality AYCE sushi

Highly recommend their salmon nigiri

Hanabusa Cafe

Only Japanese souffle pancake place that I've seen so far in Chicago/surrounding suburbs-really nicely made though!

Strings Ramen

I've eaten at both their Chinatown and Naperville lcoations. Personally prefer the Chinatow3n location.

They have a spicy Hell Ramen Challenge for anyone who thinks they can finish the bowl in half an hour!

There are levels 1-5 for the spice level of the Hell Ramen, with 5 being the Challenge level. I've gone up to Level 4...

Mochinut

They've expanded a decent bit, love their variety of mochi donut flavors!
